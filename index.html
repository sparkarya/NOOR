Our Story

Rooted in tradition,
made for today

NOOR was born from a simple belief: that clothing should feel like a second skin — beautiful, intentional, and made to last. Every stitch honours the artisans who bring each design to life.

We work with small family workshops across Rajasthan, Lucknow, and Bengal, combining age-old techniques with modern silhouettes.

Our Craft

How every piece is made

01

Design

Each collection starts with a story. Our designers sketch by hand, drawing from India's diverse textile heritage.

02

Source

We visit our artisan partners directly. Fabrics are handpicked — khadi, silk, linen, and organic cotton only.

03

Craft

Block printing, hand embroidery, and natural dyeing take days. Every detail is checked by the master artisan.

04

Deliver

Packed in recycled tissue, shipped to your door. Reuse the cloth bag — it's meant to last as long as the garment.
